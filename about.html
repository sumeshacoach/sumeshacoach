About Eesha Salvi

Eesha Salvi is the founder and heart behind Sumesha. With over 1500 international clients, she has spent years guiding people toward a balanced and fulfilling lifestyle—physically, mentally, and emotionally.

Eesha is not just a certified lifestyle coach; she is a compassionate professional who deeply understands the importance of creating change that feels achievable. Her approach is rooted in simplicity, support, and sustainable transformation. Whether through training, therapy, or nutrition, Eesha’s mission is to help individuals become the best version of themselves.

What makes her truly exceptional is not just her qualifications but her empathy, vision, and unwavering dedication to those she serves. She believes that everyone deserves to feel good, live fully, and thrive—and she has dedicated her life to making that possible for people across the world.

Leading you to the best lifestyle is not just a goal; it's our mission.
