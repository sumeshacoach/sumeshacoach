About Me

Hi, I’m Eesha Salvi, the founder and coach behind Sumesha Wellness. I'm a certified lifestyle coach with a passion for guiding people toward their best lives — physically, mentally, and emotionally.

With over 1,500 international clients, I’ve dedicated my life to helping individuals transform their routines, shift their mindset, and step into the healthiest version of themselves. At Sumesha, our goal is simple: support real people in making real changes, one step at a time.

Why Choose Me?

I understand that everyone’s journey is different. That’s why I provide coaching that’s not just about workouts or diets — it’s about understanding your lifestyle, goals, and challenges.

What sets me apart is the blend of global experience, deep empathy, and practical strategies. I’ve worked with diverse people from different cultures and age groups, and I bring that knowledge into every session.

My Journey

My fitness journey began in 2019 when I earned my international Zumba instructor license. From there, I pursued certification as a personal trainer and started applying my knowledge by working on luxury cruise ships around the world.

Traveling the globe, I realized that fitness isn't just about movement — it’s about purpose, mindset, and emotional strength. I saw how powerful it is when people believe in themselves — and I knew I had to help more people find that power within.

So I shifted my focus to my true calling: creating accessible wellness solutions for anyone who wants to feel better, look better, and live stronger. Today, through Sumesha Wellness, I work for the people — and with the people — to transform lifestyles in a sustainable, joyful, and meaningful way.
