1-on-1 Personal Training

Transform your body and confidence with personalized, one-on-one fitness sessions tailored to your lifestyle and goals. Whether you're just starting out or have experience, I create a training experience that meets you where you are—and takes you where you want to go.

Specializing in Pilates, yoga, strength training, and HIIT, I build versatile workouts that blend strength, flexibility, mindfulness, and stamina. You’ll never feel bored or stuck—we keep your sessions exciting, intentional, and full of results.

What makes Sumesha different? I don’t just train clients—I guide real people through real change. My clients often describe me as intuitive, encouraging, and driven to bring out the best in them. You’ll gain more than fitness—you’ll gain clarity, consistency, and inner strength.

Online Therapy Sessions

Your emotions deserve care, and your mind deserves peace. My online therapy sessions offer a safe, judgment-free space to explore your feelings, untangle your thoughts, and strengthen your emotional foundation—right from the comfort of your own space.

Each session is led with empathy, structure, and active support. You’ll learn to process emotions, manage stress, and rediscover the strength within. Whether you’re dealing with stress, trauma, or burnout, I’m here to help you move through it gently and confidently.

Even in a first session, many clients say they feel truly seen and supported. I believe healing starts with connection—and that’s what I offer you. Together, we’ll build emotional clarity, peace of mind, and a sense of inner home you can return to anytime.

Customized Nutrition Planning

We’ll talk about your health goals, preferences, energy levels, and challenges—and then build a smart plan that works for you. Whether you're aiming to manage weight, build muscle, or improve gut health, I’ll help you choose better, eat smarter, and feel amazing doing it.

This is a coaching experience that encourages you, adapts to you, and celebrates every milestone with you. No stress. No guilt. Just food that fits your life and makes you thrive.
